## 0.1.0

- Initial version.
- Supports reading and writing the following formats
    - MP3 (.mp3)
        - ID3v1
        - ID3v2.2
        - ID3v2.3
        - ID3v2.4
    - M4A/AAC (.m4a, .aac)
        - MP4 atoms
        - ID3 tags
    - WAV (.wav)
        - ID3v2
        - RIFF INFO
    - OPUS (.opus)
        - Vorbis Comments
